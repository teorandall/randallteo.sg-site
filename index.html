About Randall

Planning that starts with family, clarity and peace of mind.

Randall Teo helps individuals and families across Singapore plan for the future with clarity and confidence. His work focuses on practical estate and legacy planning — helping clients think through who should receive what, who should act on their behalf, and how important wishes should be documented.

His focus includes Will writing, Lasting Power of Attorney planning, estate distribution conversations and legacy planning for families who want to reduce uncertainty before life changes happen.

For Randall, good planning is not just about assets. It is about protecting loved ones from confusion, conflict and unnecessary stress.

How we'll work together

A clear path, from first conversation to signed instructions.

01

Discovery conversation

We start with an honest, no-pressure conversation about your family, assets, concerns and the people you want to protect.

02

Map out your wishes

We identify the key decisions: beneficiaries, executors, guardians where relevant, donees, distribution wishes and practical instructions.

03

Put it in place

We put the right documents and instructions in place step by step, with everything explained in plain language and no jargon.

04

Review, as life changes

Marriage, children, a new property, business changes or family changes — revisit the plan whenever life moves forward.
